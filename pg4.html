I am an artist that loves to tell stories through my art. Most of my work centers around illustrations and graphic design. Whenever I start a new project, I always think about what I am trying to communicate to the observer and how I should go about doing so. From the look of my work, you can tell that hierarchy of scale is a major tool I use to communicate to the viewer what they should be looking at. In addition, having a balanced color palette in my artwork is something I strive for since the use of color can make an image stand out more.
